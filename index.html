About

EmpEx, the Empire City Elixir Conference, is a new conference for the Elixir programming language and ecosystem held in New York City. Our goal is to bring a sense of aesthetic and fun to the growing Elixir community.

The Halloween Lightning Talks are an evening costume party featuring fun and several short technical talks. Costumes strongly encouraged.

Location

EmpEx Halloween Lightning Talks will be held at Subculture, an intimate jazz club in Soho.
45 Bleecker St, New York City, 10012
